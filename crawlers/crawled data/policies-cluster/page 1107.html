Privacy & Cookies Policy

Lasted updated on 10 April 2021

This is the Privacy & Cookies Policy (the Policy) of Hutch Games Ltd (Hutch). This Policy describes the ways in which Hutch collects and uses information about you when you visit Hutchs website at www.hutch.io (the Website) or when you play one of Hutchs games (each a Game and together the Games) on a browser, through a social networking site or as an app. It also details how Hutch collects and uses information about you when making a complaint to Hutch by e-mail, as well as if you choose to make a job application.

Hutch may change this Policy at any time and when this happens we will notify you of any changes to this Policy by noting this on our Website as well as within the Games. The changes will apply to your use of the Website and/or our Games after we have notified you. If you do not wish to accept the new Policy you should stop using the Website and/or playing our Games (as applicable). If you continue to use the Website and/or play our Games after the changes, your continued use of the Website and/or your continued playing of any of our Games shows us your agreement to be bound by the new Policy.

For the purposes of data protection legislation, Hutch is the controller for the processing of your personal data. Hutch has notified the Information Commissioners Office (ICO) of its processing of personal data. The notification indicates what data is processed by Hutch and for what purpose, and to which persons or entities the data will be provided. Hutchs registration number is ZA369120.

If you have any queries about this Policy, please contact Hutch using the contact details set out at paragraph 12 below.

1 What Personal Data is and Who this Policy applies to

Personal Data means information that identifies, relates to, is capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular person or household, including identifiers such as a real name, alias, a device identification number, location data, an online identifier, commercial information (including products or services purchased), or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of that natural person, or as further defined under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) (as applicable). We indicate in this Policy the information we collect from you.

By playing the Games or visiting the Website you are agreeing to be bound by this Policy in respect of the information collected about you. Hutch may change this Policy at any time. Hutch shall deem your continued use of the Games or access of the Website as indicating your consent to such changes. This Policy applies to the following people:

A. visitors to the Website (including those submitting a job application);

B. people who use the Games; and

C. people who make complaints to us by email.

2 Visitors to the Website

The Website is essentially a brochure for Hutchs business, its Games and opportunities to work with and for Hutch. The Website collects information from you in the following ways:

A. Hutch uses cookies for the limited purposes set out in TABLE ONE below to collect certain analytical/technical information. Hutch relies on consent to collect this information, such consent obtained via you clicking to agree to such cookies being placed when you first visit the Website or otherwise changing the cookie settings so that such cookies are not placed.

B. Should you choose to contact Hutch via the Website, Hutch will collect your e-mail address together with any other information that you submit in connection with such contact request. Hutch relies on consent to collect this information because you choose whether to contact Hutch or not and can choose how much personal information to provide to Hutch in doing so.

C. Hutch often advertises jobs on the Website. If you choose to respond to any such advertisement, or speculatively submit your CV and details for consideration through the recruitment enquiries section on the Website, any information provided by you to Hutch in connection with such response will be used to assess you as a candidate and undertake criminal record and/or checks with references. Personal information about an unsuccessful candidate shall generally be held for twelve (12) months after the recruitment exercise has been completed after which it shall be destroyed or deleted without further notice unless Hutch reasonably considers that such candidate may be a good candidate for a future role in which case Hutch shall hold such personal information for so long as is reasonable in relation to such information. Hutch may retain anonymised statistical information about applicants to help inform Hutchs recruitment activities in the future. Hutch relies on either: (i) compliance with a legal obligation; or (ii) the legitimate interests processing grounds in order to collect the information set out in this paragraph. Where legitimate interests are relied upon, it is in Hutchs legitimate interests to collect such items of personal data because this is necessary in order to carry out certain specific employment related duties relating to your application for employment with Hutch. The items of personal data collected are not of a nature whereby your rights and freedoms as a data subject are outweighed by such data collection.

3 People who contact or make complaints to Hutch by email

When Hutch receives complaints by email it shall file any relevant document and may retain details of the complainant, and other individuals identified in the complaint. Hutch will only use this information to process the complaint and to check on Hutchs services and staff.

Hutch shall retain information in relation to a complaint for a maximum of six (6) years after its closure, in a secure environment and access to it will be restricted on a need to know basis. Hutch relies on the legitimate interests processing ground to collect the information set out in this paragraph.

It is in Hutchs legitimate interests to collect such items of personal data listed here because this is necessary in order to analyse and respond to your complaint.

The items of personal data collected are not of a nature whereby your rights and freedoms as a data subject are outweighed by such data collection.

4 People who use the Games

A. Game Data:

Hutch will collect the following information from you when you use the Games:

i. If you dont log in via a social network, certain information collected by Hutchs use of cookies and similar technologies (Technologies). These collect information such as your device ID and other technical/analytical information relating to how you interact and play the Game in question. Hutch will also assign you a User-ID which is a random string of characters used to identify you as a unique player going forward. The Technologies that Hutch places when you use the Games are detailed in TABLE 2 below (Analytics Data);

ii. If you sign into one of the Games through Facebook, Google or Apple , in addition to the Analytics Data above, Hutch will also collect such information as Facebook, Google or Apple may allow Hutch to access, namely your full name, and if you sign in using Facebook, your Facebook ID. If you use any of the Game features such as chat rooms and/or support services, Hutch will also collect such information as you submit via such feature (Player Data which, together with the Analytics Data is the Game Data);

iii. if you make any purchases, Hutch will collect certain information in relation to your purchases (please see paragraph 4(b) below) ("Finance Data");

iv. in F1 Manager, if you opt-in to do so, information including your name, date of birth and e-mail address which is then used by F1 to provide you with a newsletter and other marketing information related to F1 (F1 Data);

v. if you consent or where we are otherwise permitted to send such communications in accordance with applicable data protection and electronic marketing legislation, to provide you with a newsletter and other marketing information which we consider you may be interested in (for example in relation to ours or our groups other games) (Marketing Data); and

vi. in certain instances Hutch may use certain information relating to your Game progress and/or Game spend and/or other Game activity to invite you to complete surveys in relation to its Games, including, for example, to join its Player Council and complete any other documents required for you to join (Survey Data).

B. Financial Information:

Your purchase and in-app purchases within the Games are handled through the platform on which you purchased the Game or through other payment processors (e.g. the Apple App Store, Google Play, xBox Live, Playstation Network). Hutch does not retain any card or payment details, although Hutch does keep a record of your purchases and your aggregate spending.

C. What if Hutch cannot collect this information:

You can use your browser settings and privacy settings on your social networks to prevent us collecting certain of the above information about you (see, for example, paragraphs 5(f) and 12 below). If you still have concerns about the information we collect please contact us at any time by sending an e-mail to support@hutchgames.com.

D. Usage by us:

The categories of personal data mentioned above and otherwise herein have been collected and used by us in the past twelve (12) months for the business purpose(s) indicated herein.

I. in relation to the Survey Data for Hutch to provide such survey to you; and

J. share with third parties for the purposes as described in paragraph 7 below.

14 Cookies and other similar technologies

A. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a Website. Other similar technologies, defined in this policy as Technologies, are third party technologies used in a Game.

Hutchs use of cookies and Technologies is detailed below.

Session cookies enable you to move from page to page within websites, and any information you enter will be remembered but is deleted when you close your browser after a short time.

Persistent cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings when you browse the Website in the future, in different browser sessions.

We may use your social login (if you access a Game via a social media platform) and/or Hutch User ID for save protection so that you can restore your progress in a Game.

In our Games we use Technologies that track or store your data for advertising, attribution or analytics purposes.

B. Disabling Cookies:

The use of the cookies and Technologies described above improves the functionality of the Games and the Website and your experience using them.

If you do not want these cookies and/or Technologies to be served on your device, you are able to disable them by changing the settings on your browser, or on your device.

Please note that if you do decide to disable cookies and/or Technologies you may not be able to access some of the Games or the Website, some of the features of the Games or the Website, or the Games or Website may not function properly.

By continuing to use the Games, and/or the Website, including using social networking media functionality, you consent to the relevant cookies and/or Technologies being set on your device.
